2023-Present: Master 2 in 3IR

Le Master mention “ingénierie et innovation en images et réseaux” (M3IR) a pour objectif d’offrir une formation en traitement d’images et réseaux informatiques, à finalité professionnelle ou recherche, selon deux parcours (images, réseaux).

La deuxième année (M2) propose des enseignements de préparation à l’insertion professionnelle. Le parcours “réseaux” s’oriente vers les technologies réseaux et le développement d’applications Internet. L’objectif est de fournir aux étudiants les bases théoriques et la maîtrise pratique des technologies liées au multimédia et aux réseaux informatiques, afin de former de futurs professionnels destinés au marché du secteur industriel du transport et du traitement de l’information audio-visuelle et du multimédia.

2018-2023: Computer and Communication Engineering

It is one of the first faculties in Applied Sciences at the Lebanese University which is a public, co-educational institution of higher education founded in 1974 by Presidential Decree number 9305 (21 October 1974).

Presently, the Engineering Faculty has five departments: the Common Core, Civil Engineering, Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Chemical Engineering Departments. The language of instruction is French or English depending on the choice of the students in the Common Core program and on the background of the professors in the three following specialty years.

The Faculty offers programs leading to the following diplomas:

  1. An Engineering Diploma after completion of five academic years of successful study in the faculty (two years of common core engineering + three years of engineering major),totaling to 10 semesters, the last semester of which is dedicated to the final year project. The curriculum of the Engineering degree requires the completion of 24 credits per semester, that is an overall total of 240 credits. There is a short break between fall and spring semesters and a two-months summer break between the spring and the fall semesters.
  2. A Master’s degree after one year, i.e.2 semesters of successful study following the Engineering Diploma degree.
  3. Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) degree in Engineering is delivered by the Doctoral School of Sciences and Technology (Ecole Doctorale des Sciences et Technologie EDST) at the Lebanese University.
